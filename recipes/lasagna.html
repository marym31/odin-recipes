Lasagna

Description:

This rich and tasty dish originates from the Emilia region, specifically, from the city of Bologna. Lasagna, however, is known, appreciated, made, remade, tasted and shared not only throughout Italy, but also abroad as an Italian symbolic dish.

Layers of green egg pasta, dressed with the classic traditional ragù, béchamel and grated cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Beef 10.6 oz (300 g) - (minced, coarsely ground and mixed)
  • Carrots ½ cup (50 g)
  • Yellow onions 1 cup (50 g)
  • Tomato puree 1 ¼ cup (300 g)
  • Pancetta bacon 5.3 oz (150 g)
  • Celery ½ cup (50 g)
  • Red wine ½ cup (100 g)
  • Extra virgin olive oil 1 tbsp
  • Béchamel (500 g)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. To prepare lasagne alla Bolognese, start with the meat sauce. Prepare the vegetable broth and ensure to keep it warm. Then take the pancetta bacon, cut it into strips first and then chop it well. Separately, finely chop the carrots, onions, celery and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, pour a drizzle of oil and the pancetta bacon. Use a ladle to spread it well and let it brown for a few minutes. Then add the chopped vegetables and cook for 5-6 minutes. Then add the minced meat. Stir and raise the heat. Let the meat brown slowly, it must be well browned to seal the juices and be soft.
  3. Deglaze with red wine and let it evaporate completely before adding the tomato puree. The Bolognese sauce must cook for two hours. When it starts boiling again, you can add some hot broth, one or two ladles, and let the sauce cook for another couple of hours at least.
  4. Take a large 12x8 inches (30x20 cm) lasagna pan and grease the bottom. Add a thin layer of béchamel and one of Bolognese sauce. Then place the first sheet,add a layer of béchamel and a layer of meat sauce. Add grated Parmesan cheese and place another sheet of pasta on top.
  5. Add another layer of béchamel and one of meat sauce, add grated cheese and continue like this until you have 5 layers. After placing the last sheet, add the Bolognese sauce.
